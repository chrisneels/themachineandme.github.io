polis partners with homeowners by buying equity in their homes.

we're reimagining home ownership for Canadians.

Polis is an alternative to home equity loans and HELOCs. Instead of lending you money, we buy a stake in your home today for a share in the future appreciation or depreciation of your home.

You can choose to buy back our stake whenever you like, or when you eventually sell your home. We’re reimagining home ownership in Canada by being a true partner to you: we do well when you do well, and we share in some of the risk in property.

Now, rather than choosing between owning 0% (renting) or 100% (buying), you have a better option.

how polis helps.

Using the capital raised from Polis, you can responsibly lower your cost of home ownership and reduce some of the risks of home ownership. Here are a few ways that the investment from Polis could help you:

  • Reduce your interest payments by paying down your mortgage.
  • Pay down credit card debt and other outstanding loans.
  • Invest in home improvements without taking on additional debt.
  • Diversify your portfolio by investing in alternative asset classes.

    Homeownership can be tough. Rising housing prices have caused mounting levels of Canadian household debt, with the ratio of debt to disposable income reaching a record high of 167.3% in 2017. Homeowners often have little choice but to borrow large sums to afford their homes, which can be costly and risky. For example a 2017 Manulife Study reported that 70% of mortgage holders are not able to manage a ten per cent increase in their payments.

    As Rob Carrick writes in The Globe and Mail, “Rising house prices make people wealthier on paper, but they also threaten to seriously damage the personal finances of those who borrow more than they can truly afford to buy a home. While most Canadians can handle their payments, the ranks of those suffering financial stress from oversized debt is growing, and quickly.”
