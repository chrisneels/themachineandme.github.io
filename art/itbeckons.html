The Machine and Me ⬅

It Beckons

To the pre-thought notifs, you respond without a thought.

To be human is to bond with things in the physical world. Once a thing (the device) takes a life of its own, your relation with it shifts. When it stirs with chimes and micromovements, and when it doesn’t, you react voluntarily and involuntarily (i.e., Skinnerian and Pavlonian conditioning, respectively). And when you wake, live, and sleep, the device is everpresent.
